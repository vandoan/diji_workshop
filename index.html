Work and Co
Fonts, spacing, color palatte, transitions, and more.

After a lengthy process, Work and Co, a digital product agency, completed the new design for The Week's website. With a small and agile team and while working to support ad campaigns and another large website, we were able to quickly build and deliver the new website at the beginning of 2015.
